Wind Speed

These charts were created using wind measurements from all of the locations above, to find a general trend over the entire country.
The speed of the wind in Australia follows a cyclic pattern annually, with the lowest wind speed in winter, and the highest in spring. Use the slider below the line chart to see the pattern over the years.
In general, the wind speed is clustered around 12-13km/h, and rarely exceeds 25/km/h.
